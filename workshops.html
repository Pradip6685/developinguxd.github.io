Workshop "Developing UXD"

We just came back from Berlin, where we hosted the biggest workshop of the "IA Konferenz 2014".

16 UX Designers (some of them with no frontend knowledge at all) wrapped their heads about the basics and best practices of HTML, CSS and Bootstrap.

We got the feedback, that this was a great start for becoming a "Coding UX Designer", but we all know that — of course — the biggest chunk of work is still ahead of us. Follow us on Twitter or join us on Facebook to get updates about our project!

If you are also interested in getting started with "Developing UXD", feel free to contact us.

Content of the workshop

We offer individual workshops based on your needs.

This is what we have planned so far :

  • Why you should build wireframes and designs in the browser
  • What could be a new workflow
  • Introduction to HTML and HTML5
  • Introduction to CSS
  • Working with Chrome Developer Tools
  • Getting started with Bootstrap
  • Next steps for learning
  • Knowledge and learning resources
