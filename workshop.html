Workshop "Developing UXD"
@ IA Konferenz 2014

We have a workshop! If you want to take part, jump over to iakonferenz.org and register.

What you will learn

Our workshop is just half a day, so we are a bit in a hurry to get you on track.

This is what we have planned so far :

  • Why you should build wireframes and designs in the browser
  • What could be a new workflow
  • Introduction to HTML and HTML5
  • Introduction to CSS
  • Chrome Developer Tools
  • Bootstrap
  • Knowledge and learning resources
Feel free to contact us during the conference as well, if you want to participate in this project!

Bring your computer

If you participate in our workshop, please bring your Mac with preinstalled Chrome and Sublime Text 3
